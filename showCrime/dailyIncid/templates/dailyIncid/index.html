Visualizing Oakland Police Department
Daily Incident data

If you have any questions about OakCrime.org, send a message to info@oakcrime.org. Please include which beat / NCPC you live in (cf. OPD District & Beat Locator), and a description of your intended use of the facility, so we can plan accordingly.

  • Heatmap overview

    "Heatmap" of last 90 days of crime. Zoom map, select crime categories and date range of interest, and zoom into questions of interest. Heatmap gives way to individual markers for each crime as you get a smaller set.

  • Plot longitudinal data

    All data (since 2014) for a specific OPD "beat" and one or two crime categories

  • Gun-related crimes

    Crimes involving guns, within the last 90 days, based on patrol daily log entries.

  • Plot recent incidents near a geographic point on a map:

    All incidents within the last six months and within a 0.5 kilometer radius of the point of interested are plotted on a map. "Major" crime incidents (HOMOCIDE, SEX_RAPE, WEAPONS, ROBBERY, ASSAULT) are plotted with red markers; all other incidents are shown as green markers.

    • Incidents near an Oakland BART station

    • Incidents near a (District 4) Oakland park

    • Incidents near a specific location (with known longitude/latitude)

  • Or try out access to the data via our API:

    All queries begin with the same oakcrime.org/dailyIncid prefix, followed by one of three different strings:

    • beatAPI/03X/

      crimes from specified beat (eg 03X); restricted to last two years

    • crimeCatAPI/LARCENY_THEFT_VEHICLE_AUTO

      crimes for some crime category (eg LARCENY_THEFT_VEHICLE_AUTO); restricted to last 6 months

    • nearHereAPI/-122.265944_37.828199

      crimes within 500m of this longitude_latitude; restricted to last 6 months
